1. Encephalon  

Encephalon is a virtual consultation engine developed with the motivation to act as a first form of contact for students who feel like they are under too much pressure, stress, anxiety, or feel like their mental health is being neglected.

2. MEKO  

MEKO serves to be a mockup for an NFT marketplace made entirely of guess what? CATS!!! Chekout the video for the NFT on the website if you like cats, I can assure you, you're in for a treat!

3. Streak-0-Meter  

Streak-0-Meter is a visually pleasing streak tracker app to keep track of your daily goals. It has 5 beautiful gradients to choose from and lets you update the counter once a day.

4. Brandon (Lego EV3)

Meet 'Brandon' who was given to me as assignment which rekindled my passion for both football and robotics. He's a Lego EV3 Mindstorm Kit with all the bells and whistles ( IR sensor, UV sensor, Range Finder ) which makes him one of the best robot goalies out there.

5. Twitter Dataset EDA   

For this one, I was asked to conduct a preliminary analysis of a twitter user dataset. The whole task was to find find parameters to feed a classification model that discrens the gender of the person based on the tweet input and uncover hidden patterns that may prove useful. Take a look at the report and let me know what you think?
